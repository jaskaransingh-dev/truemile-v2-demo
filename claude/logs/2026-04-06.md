## Decisions made

- **Three-tier email extraction**: mailto anchors → anchor text with `@` → COMMENTS section parent textContent regex. No document.body scanning.
- **Horizontal panel buttons**: Email/Route/Calc side-by-side instead of stacked vertically. Better use of panel space.
- **Google Maps /dir/ format**: Shows leg-by-leg times (deadhead + loaded separately). Origin from DAT search bar, not popup.
- **Quick Calc uses total miles (loaded + deadhead)** for variable cost. Utilization = loaded/total × 100.
- **estimatedDays step function in client**: Same brackets as engine (<600→1d, 600–1269→2d, 1270–1799→3d, 1800–2399→4d, ≥2400→5d).
- **Auto-scroll before ranking**: scrollHeight/6 steps ensure all virtualized rows are collected before scraping.
